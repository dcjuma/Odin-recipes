chickpea salad sandwich recipe

Description

At times you are between errands and you cannot just afford to start cooking a proper or full course meal for that matter but still you are hungry and need to eat. What then? You have to order a quick bite but then time is an issue.

An easy way out is just to make a sandwich and below is the easiest way to make a delicious sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 6tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6tablespoons tahini
  • 3tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • ½teaspoon grated garlic
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2(15-ounce) cans chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • ½cup finely chopped celery
  • ½cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 8slices multigrain sandwich bread
  • Mayonnaise, as needed
  • 8butter lettuce leaves
  • 1packed cup alfalfa sprouts
  • 1avocado, thinly sliced
  • 1large tomato, thinly sliced
  • ½cup finely chopped scallions

steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine oil, tahini, lemon juice and garlic. Season with salt and pepper, then whisk until well combined. Add chickpeas and mash with the back of a fork or spoon until chunky. Add celery, scallions and cheese, and mix well. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Spread one side of each bread slice with mayonnaise. On 4 slices of bread, divide lettuce, alfalfa and avocado, then top with some of the chickpea salad and tomatoes. Close sandwiches and serve. (Sandwiches can sit at room temperature for 1 to 2 hours before serving.)
