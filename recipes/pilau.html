pilau recipe

Description

The dish combines rice with aromatic spices, chicken or beef. It adds depth to the plainness of white rice or brown rice or generally just rice as most would perceive

It is necessary to point out that it is a traditionally spicy dish and therefore might not be recommended to people with hyperacidity or other digestive problems. However, if you are clear and would like to impress with your cooking skills then be my guest.

Ingredients

  • two medium sized onions
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • cooking oil
  • powdered pilau masala
  • pilau masala whole
  • beef
  • soy sauce
  • salt
  • tomato paste

steps

  1. Start with boiling your beef for about 30minutes until its dry and has a golden brown shade.
  2. After your beef is ready, shallow fry it with some little cooking oil. Then add in your grounded garlic then ginger to add up some flavor.
  3. Add tomato paste,or blended tomatoes to the beef. Then put in some pilau whole while stirring and some salt to taste.
  4. Add the rice into the beef and some little Soy sauce to add up the brown color, stir continuously then add some pilau masala to generate the nice pilau smell.
  5. Add enough water into the rice and beef mix (pilau). Cover the cooking apparatus with a lid to allow it to cook until its ready.
