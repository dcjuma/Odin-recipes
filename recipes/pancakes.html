pancakes recipe

Description

Most people consider pancakes a breakfast meal but the truth of the matter is that they are just as a good a meal as any other. Meaning they can be consumed at any time of your liking and are just as satisfactory.

Furthermore, they can be accompanied by any drink that you choose; the tea, coffee, juice or even beer lovers. So join me in this brief journey as I take you through making life changing pancakes for you.

Ingredients

  • flour: This quick pancake recipe starts with a cup of all-purpose flour.
  • sugar: You will need two tablespoons of white sugar.
  • baking powder: Baking powder acts as a leavening agent, ensuring perfectly fluffy pancakes.
  • salt: Salt enhances the overall flavor of the pancakes, but it will not make the pancakes taste salty.
  • milk and oil: Milk and oil add moisture and flavor to the pancake batter.
  • egg: A beaten egg lends more moisture and helps bind the batter together.

steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the wet ingredients and mix.
  3. Pour or ladle the batter onto the oiled griddle or pan.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook on the other side.
